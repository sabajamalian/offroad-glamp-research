- **Most Vancouver‑area off‑road products are day‑trips** (ATV/buggy tours) with pricing clustered around **$199–$339** per rider/buggy or **$800** per private group day. ## 4) Market Sizing (Vancouver + BC Adventure Tourism)

- **Vancouver visitor volume & spend:**
  - **2023:** ~**10.95M** overnight visitors; **C$8.4B** visitor spending.  
  - **2024:** ~**11.27M** overnight visitors; **C$9.6B** visitor spending.  
  [Source](https://roadgenius.com/statistics/tourism/canada/vancouver/) 

- **BC adventure tourism market size:**
  - **Adventure tourism revenue (2023): ~C$10.7B** in BC.  
  - **Outdoor recreation total revenue (2023): ~C$17B**; **GDP contribution: C$4.8B**.  
  [Destination BC sector profile](https://www.destinationbc.ca/content/uploads/2025/10/Destination-BC-Sector-Profile-Outdoor-Recreation_FINAL.pdf) · [BIV summary](https://www.biv.com/small-business/bc-outdoor-tourism-sector-worth-at-least-48b-says-report-11482085)

- **Seasonality patterns:**
  - Most adventure tours operate **spring–fall**, with peak demand in summer (e.g., rafting runs **April–September**). [Squamish Rafting](https://squamish-rafting.com/day-trips/)

- **Customer demographics (inferred from operator requirements):**
  - Family‑friendly adventures start at **age 5+** (rafting), while **drivers are 19+** for ATV and **21+** for some off‑road tours. [Rafting age](https://squamish-rafting.com/day-trips/) · [ATV driver minimums](https://www.canadianwilderness.com/whistler/atv-tours/) · [Vancouver Outdoor Adventures age requirement](https://vancouveroutdoors.ca/services/vancouver-offroad-atv-tour-4/)

## 5) Gaps & Opportunities

1. **Guided overnight off‑road + camping is scarce near Vancouver.** Current direct competitors are either **day‑only** ATV/buggy tours or **self‑drive rentals**; guided multi‑day options are mainly **Vancouver Island‑based**. This leaves room for a **Vancouver‑centric overnight guided off‑road + camping package**. [Day tours](https://vancouveroutdoors.ca/services/vancouver-offroad-atv-tour-4/) · [Self‑drive rentals](https://faroutwilderness.com/reservations/) · [Vancouver Island tours](https://www.westernredadventures.ca/tour-packages)
2. **Pricing gap for mid‑range multi‑day experiences.** Day tours run **~$199–$339** per rider/buggy, while rentals are **$175–$305/day**. There is little in‑market pricing for **2‑day guided, all‑inclusive** overland experiences near Vancouver. [ATV pricing](https://www.canadianwilderness.com/whistler/atv-tours/) · [Buggy pricing ref](https://www.whistlerblackcomb.com/explore-the-resort/activities-and-events/summer-activities/rzr-tours.aspx) · [Rental pricing](https://faroutwilderness.com/reservations/)
3. **Experience gap:** operators don’t combine **hands‑on off‑road driving** with a **curated overnight camp** (gear setup, meals, stargazing, local storytelling). This is a clear differentiation point versus glamping and day‑tour competition. [Glamping market](https://www.hipcamp.com/en-CA/d/canada/british-columbia/vancouver/camping/glamping)
4. **Underserved segments:** small groups seeking **private, guided, beginner‑friendly off‑road** plus **comfort‑level camping** could be targeted with bundled pricing and transportation from Vancouver.
