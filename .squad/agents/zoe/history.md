# zoe — Session History

## Project Context
- **Owner:** Saba
- **Business:** Off-road driving experience + car camping in Vancouver BC area
- **Stack:** Research & business planning (document deliverables)
- **Concept:** Taking up to 5 guests outside Vancouver BC for off-road driving with Toyota vehicles, overnight stays with rooftop tents
- **Deliverables:** Competitive Analysis document and Business Plan document

## Learnings
- Direct competitors in Vancouver/Sea‑to‑Sky: Vancouver Outdoor Adventures (private $800 group day tour), Canadian Wilderness Adventures (Whistler ATV tours ~$199–$219/driver), Squamish Adventure (buggy tours; local pricing ~$315–$339/buggy), Maple Overland ($225/night Jeep w/ rooftop tent), FarOut Wilderness ($175–$305/day 4x4 camper rentals), Western Red Adventures (guided convoy tours; price by inquiry).
