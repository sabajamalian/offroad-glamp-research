## Learnings
*   **Vancouver Market Nuance:** The local market is highly active and outdoorsy but saturated. Differentiation must come from the "unique access" (off-road capability) and "comfort" (glamping) blend.
*   **Cost Per Acquisition:** Industry benchmarks suggest Google Ads CPA can be $50-$70, while Social CPA is lower ($5-$25) but requires better creative.
*   **Budgeting:** A 15-20% revenue allocation for marketing in the first 6 months is aggressive but necessary to break through the noise.
*   **Channel Hierarchy:** Instagram > SEO > Partnerships > Paid Ads.
