# kaylee — Session History

## Project Context
- **Owner:** Saba
- **Business:** Off-road driving experience + car camping in Vancouver BC area
- **Stack:** Research & business planning (document deliverables)
- **Concept:** Taking up to 5 guests outside Vancouver BC for off-road driving with Toyota vehicles, overnight stays with rooftop tents
- **Deliverables:** Competitive Analysis document and Business Plan document

## Learnings
- **Fastest launch path:** Squarespace Commerce Basic paired with Acuity Growing covers group booking, deposits, and CAD payments with ~$972/year in fixed costs, making it ideal for validating demand before investing in custom builds.
- **Scalable alternatives:** WordPress (SiteGround + WooCommerce Bookings) stays under ~$500 in year one but requires more maintenance; Wix Business ($477/year) includes Wix Bookings, while Shopify Basic + Experiences averages $1,056/year once app fees are included.
- **Enterprise booking systems:** Checkfront ($149/mo + 3%) and Rezdy ($49–$249/mo + 3%) provide waivers, OTA distribution, and API access; FareHarbor/Peek rely on 6–8% booking fees that can be passed to guests but can hurt conversion.
